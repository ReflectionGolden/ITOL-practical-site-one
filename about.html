About Magium

Magium is a Choose Your Own Adventure story game which is centered around a normal person named Barry who has entered himself into a mage tournament with the goal to get his hands on the grand prize and achieve his dream of mecoming a mage. Throughout his journey he makes new friends, new enemies, and learns much about both himself and some ancient history which becomes all too relevant to upcoming events.

You have the chance to make choices that effect how the story plays out in various ways, such as the chance to help different characters or choose how you act in different fights to change their outcomes. There are many different strategies that you can make use of to progress the story in your own way.

Are you able to guide Barry through this deadly tournament?
