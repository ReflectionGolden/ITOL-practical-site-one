This is the main Community Discord dedicated to Magium, with many channels for different purposes including a channel that is linked to the @Magium_Games twitter account in order to provide updates on when each chapter was posted while the original author was still making the game.

This is the official Discord server fot the Magium Writer Team who have taken up the mantle of recreating the game for people to play since the original app was taken off of the android and apple app stores and once that is done they plan to continue writing the story from Book 4 onwards.
